About Us

Congratulations for being accepted to pursue your higher degree at the North-West University (NWU). NWU is one of the top ranked universities in terms of its cutting-edge research. To maintain this trajectory, the Centre for Teaching and Learning provides you with research teaching support to develop critical research skills required for timely completion of your postgraduate qualification.

Welcome to the Postgraduate Research Support for Enhanced Throughput Hub, a platform where you find all the research support you need to improve the quality of your research output as well as your experience as a postgraduate researcher at NWU. On this site, you will meet your peers as well as Postgraduate Research Advising Specialist who will assist you address any challenge you might have with your research. You will also meet other students and engage in a range of pedagogic encounters, and find all the necessary information you need to know at all critical junctures and levels of the postgraduate study

Projects support activities

Workshops

A series of workshops aimed at developing research skills among pipeline postgraduate students are presented throughout the year. These begin with an invitational Thesis and Dissertation Writing Workshop that traverses practical and conceptual aspects that constitute a guide to constructing and timely completion of a good dissertation. Follow-up workshops are then developed to address common areas of concern identified during the course of the year.

Walk in research support hubs

As a postgraduate student, you have an opportunity to visit a research support hub for a group or individual one-on-one consultation with a Research Advising Specialist on any aspect of your research. Voluntary consultations may be self-initiated or referrals from your supervisor. Physical or virtual consultations can be made at your campus or, depending on expertise or availability of the specialist, at any of the three NWU campuses after prior booking.

Writing camps

Postgraduate students can get support at our writing camps for finalising their thesis or dissertation and for boosting publications as part of their postgraduate study journey.
